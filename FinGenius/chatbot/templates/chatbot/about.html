About FinGenius

FinGenius is a financial advice chatbot created by Alex Sanchez, a student at Reinhardt University, as part of the Web Application Development class. This application aims to help users make informed financial decisions through interactive conversations.

Functionalities

  • Interactive Chatbot: Users can engage with FinGenius to receive personalized financial advice, tips on budgeting, and investment strategies.
  • User Authentication: Users can create accounts and log in to access the chatbot, ensuring a secure and personalized experience.
  • Feedback System: Users can provide feedback on the chatbot's responses to help improve its accuracy and effectiveness.
  • Responsive Design: The application is designed to be user-friendly on both desktop and mobile devices.

With FinGenius, managing your finances has never been easier! Whether you're looking for budgeting tips or investment advice, our chatbot is here to assist you every step of the way.
